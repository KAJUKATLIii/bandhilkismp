Official Rulebook

Please read and understand all rules before applying for the whitelist. Violations may result in warnings, mutes, kicks, or bans.

  • Respect every player regardless of rank or role.
  • No toxicity, hate speech, threats, or harassment of any kind.
  • No drama creation or provoking other players.
  • English or Hindi only in global chat.
  • Staff instructions must be followed at all times without argument.
  • No griefing, stealing from other players, or raiding bases.
  • No hacking, x-ray texture packs, kill aura, or unfair client mods.
  • No duplication glitches or exploiting game mechanics.
  • AFK farms must comply with server-defined limits.
  • Do not trap or intentionally harm new players.
  • Respect other players' builds, bases, and claimed land.
  • Do not build too close to another player's base without explicit permission.
  • No inappropriate or offensive builds of any kind.
  • Public community builds must be clearly marked and announced.
  • Scamming players in trades or economy transactions is strictly prohibited.
  • Do not exploit economy bugs, glitches, or loop-holes.
  • Selling in-game items for real-world money (RWT) is not allowed.
  • Only approved client mods are allowed (e.g., Optifine, Sodium, minimaps without player/cave radar).
  • Macro scripts that automate gameplay (like auto-clicking or auto-farming) are forbidden.
  • Using alternative accounts (alts) to bypass bans or limits is not permitted.
  • Town leaders are responsible for the actions of their members inside town borders.
  • Do not claim land within 500 blocks of another town without their permission.
  • Declaring war or hostilities must be approved by staff or mutually agreed upon.
  • Bases are considered "abandoned" only after 30 days of owner inactivity.
  • You must receive staff permission via a ticket before looting an abandoned base.
  • Do not completely destroy or grief an abandoned base even if looting is approved.
  • PvP is only allowed with mutual consent from all parties involved.
  • No spawn killing or camping at another player's base.
  • Event-specific rules override normal server rules during active events.
  • No spamming messages or mic abuse in voice channels.
  • No NSFW, explicit, or inappropriate content in any channel.
  • No unsolicited advertising of other servers, services, or products.
  • Follow Discord's Terms of Service at all times.
